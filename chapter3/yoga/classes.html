Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Intended for beginners and anyone a grounded foundation in the pactice of yoga, this 60 minute class of poses and slow movement focuses on asana (proper alignment and posture), pranayama a9abreath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection.
Vinyasa Yoga
Although designed for intermediate to advenced students, beginners are welcome to sampel this 60 minutes class that focuses on breth-synchronized movement – you will inhale and exhale as you flow energetically through yoga poses.
Restorative Yoga
This 90 minutes class features very slow movement and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience. This practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury.
